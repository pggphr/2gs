About

G2 Solutions combines IT governance and financial management. This approach is built on long-term experience gained in both local and international organisations operating under high operational and regulatory standards.

G2 Solutions is led by a small, highly specialised team focused on structure, control and long-term business stability.

The objective is simple: clarity, control and long-term stability.

IT Governance and Security

Experience includes work in distributed and security-driven environments, identity and access management, system standardisation and implementation of international standards such as ISO 27001.

The focus is on documented processes, clear rules and systems that remain reliable even within complex organisational structures.

Financial Management and Control

Financial experience has been developed within both domestic and international organisations, covering management reporting, planning, cost control and cooperation with banks and auditors.

The approach is analytical and structured, with emphasis on informed decision-making.

Integrated Perspective

In practice, IT and finance often operate separately. One focuses on infrastructure and security, the other on numbers and reporting. When these areas are not aligned, operations become unclear and exposed to risk.

Bringing IT governance and financial discipline together provides a unified view of the business system, from infrastructure security to reporting quality.

G2 Solutions takes operational responsibility in the areas of IT and finance.
